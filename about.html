About.

Hi, I'm Jake

I'm a Computer Science Graduate from Visayas State University, July 2025 . I enjoy building web applications that are user-friendly and useful. In my free time, I like to code, but most of the time, you'll find me playing games.

Education

Even before college, I already aspired to become a developer, which inspired me to pursue a degree in Computer Science. I studied at Visayas State University (VSU), where I honed my skills in programming, web development, and problem-solving. My academic journey culminated with my thesis project in Forestry, which was developed in order to assist the forest guards in their work. This experience, along with my graduation, represents both my hard work and my passion for creating solutions that are practical and impactful.
